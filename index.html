About Us

IMIG is an organization open to any past or present mathematics students looking to involve themselves more in their studies or the field as a whole. IMIG is intended to host a place for young mathematicians to both help and challenge one another intellectually and conceptually; and to encourage networking and collaboration between aspiring mathematicians. We strive to help students not only explore and grow their knowledge of mathematics, but to begin preparing for a future in industrial mathematics or research.

Our Plans

IMIG plans to host many events, including career and professional workshops, research sessions, game or trivia nights, and many more. General meetings will be held twice monthly, and members will be expected to attend at least half of all meetings, barring approved exemptions. Members are expected to choose a published paper that has a strong mathematical or statistical focus. There will be a list of pre-approved papers, however papers can be approved by the Board of Directors. We encourage everyone to study and make a genuine attempt to understand their choosen paper in full and prepare a presentation over the subject matter for the end of the term to share findings with peers.
